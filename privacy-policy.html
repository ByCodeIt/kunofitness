Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

KunoFitness is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

What Information We Collect

Information you provide: When you place an order, you provide your name, phone number, and delivery address. This information is sent directly to us via WhatsApp and is not stored on any server operated by KunoFitness.

Information stored on your device: Your shopping cart is saved in your browser's local storage on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to or accessible by KunoFitness.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide at checkout is used solely to process and fulfil your order — confirming your purchase, arranging delivery, and communicating with you about your order. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your consent.

Cookies and Tracking

KunoFitness does not use cookies, analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking technologies on this website.

Your shopping cart is stored using your browser's local storage — a technology that keeps data on your own device only. This is not a cookie and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts: This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers as part of a standard HTTP request. This is governed by Google's privacy policy.

WhatsApp: Orders are placed via WhatsApp, operated by Meta. When you send your order through WhatsApp, Meta's privacy policy applies to that communication. KunoFitness does not control how Meta handles data transmitted through WhatsApp.

Data Retention

KunoFitness does not store your personal data on any server or database. Order information received via WhatsApp is retained only as long as necessary to fulfil your order and handle any related enquiries. You may request deletion of this information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request that KunoFitness delete any order information held about you. To make such a request, contact us via WhatsApp or email and we will respond promptly.

You may clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings. This will remove your saved cart from your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kunofitness@gmail.com or reach us directly on WhatsApp.
